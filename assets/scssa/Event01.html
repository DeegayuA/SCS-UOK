Sing Along With SCSSA 2023

On February 2, 2023, the Statistics and Computer Science Students' Association (SCSSA) organized an event called "Sing Along with SCSSA". The event was coordinated by Miss Layeshi and was held near the A7 building of the University. The event started at 6 pm and ended at 8 pm, and was attended by a large number of students. It was a significant event that provided an opportunity for students to showcase their musical talents and enjoy an evening of music and entertainment.

The event featured a wide range of musical performances, including solo singing, duets, and group performances. The performances were diverse, ranging from classical to contemporary, and included songs from different cultures and languages.

The event was a great success, and the audience was thoroughly entertained. The SCSSA received a lot of positive feedback from the attendees, who appreciated the effort put in by the organizers and the performers. The event provided an excellent platform for students to showcase their musical talents, and it was a great opportunity for the school community to come together and enjoy an evening of music and entertainment.
