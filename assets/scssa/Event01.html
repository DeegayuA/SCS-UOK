Sing Along With SCSSA 2023

The Statistics and Computer Science Students' Association (SCSSA) organized an event called "Sing Along with SCSSA" on February 2, 2023. The event was coordinated by Ms Layeshi Silva and was held in front of the A7 building of the University. The event started at 6 pm and ended at 8 pm, and was attended by a large number of students.

The event featured a wide range of musical performances, including solo singing, duets, and group performances. The performances were diverse, ranging from classical to contemporary, and included songs from different cultures and languages. It was a significant event that provided an opportunity for students to showcase their musical talents and enjoy an evening of music and entertainment.
