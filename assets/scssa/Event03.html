රූShine'23 - Film Festival

The film festival "රූshine'23" organized by the Statistics & Computer Science Students' Association (SCSSA). The festival was held on February 6, 7, and 8, 2023, at the Science Auditorium from 6 PM onwards.

The festival featured three blockbuster movies, including Annihilation, Arrival, and Top Gun, which captivated the audience's attention. The cost of a ticket per movie was Rs.100, and the tickets were sold outside the auditorium. In addition, there were various snacks available for purchase to enhance the movie-watching experience.

The festival attracted a large number of students, and it was an excellent opportunity for them to enjoy quality movies while spending time with their friends. Overall, the "රූShine'23'" film festival was a grand success, and the organizers deserve congratulations for their hard work and dedication.
